What is Green 360?

Green 360 is the movement for a cleaner enviroment and healthy foods through the use of organic waste. We have found that many kitchen items that were usually thrown away can be put to good use through our all natural composting methods.



Many local farms are willing to work with us to come up with a perfect blend of compost suited for your farming needs. We will simply pick up whatever fruit scraps, vegetable scraps, coffee grounds, etc. you may have once a month and deliver whatever amount of soil form your choosing directly to your house each and every month.



We would like to encourage communities all around the world to participate in our movement by practicing your own growing techniques and building your own local farms.



Find out more on what you can do to help on the What to Compost? Page.

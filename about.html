What is green 360?

Green 360 wants nothing but the best for our home farmers and will stop at nothing to ensure a greener future. For our subscription we provide monthly pickup of whatever compost you produce that would originally be thrown away. At our facilities we mix and produce the most organic high grade compost so that your plants can be as healthy as possible. When the final product is produced our drivers drop it off right at your door! If you dont want to subscribe then you can buy however much compost you need at our shop.

Why We Do It

We think composting is very important becasue of it many benefits. Composting reduces landfill waste and can divert as much as 30% of household waste away from the garbage can. That’s important because when organic matter hits the landfill, it lacks the air it needs to decompose quickly. Instead, it creates harmful methane gas as it breaks down, increasing the rate of global warming and climate change. Composting also offers a natural alternative to chemical fertilizers when applied to lawns and garden beds.
